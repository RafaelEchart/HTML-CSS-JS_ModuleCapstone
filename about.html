"Hello! Sharing world"

The FullStack Developer brings together the community of experts, academics, and activists who comprise the FullStack Developer affiliate network in a different country every two years.
This year, we hope to expand our invitation list incluing organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of the Developers, free culture and open knowledge.
So if you're active and engaged in the world of open content and knowledge - free software advocates, Wikipedians, Open Knowledge, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, governments and foundations, lawyers, and activists - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant developer together.
